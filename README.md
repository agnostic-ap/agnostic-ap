## About Me

I am a full-stack developer with 5 years of experience building production web apps, mobile apps, mini programs, automation tools, and AI-integrated workflows.  
I like turning rough product ideas into shipped software, from frontend architecture and backend integration to deployment, documentation, and iteration after launch.  
My work has covered New Zealand client projects, WeChat Mini Programs, Android real-time communication apps, commercial automation scripts, and AI-assisted product workflows.  
Right now I am shaping my GitHub around practical open-source tools, polished case studies, and developer-facing products that solve real workflow problems.
